Get HorizonOS

Flash the ISO to a USB drive and boot. Works on most x86_64 hardware from the last decade.

  • Architecture: x86_64
  • Size: ~2.4 GB (approx.)

Quick install

  1. Download the ISO and verify the SHA256 checksum.
  2. Write to USB with Balena Etcher, Rufus, or dd.
  3. Boot from USB, follow the Horizon installer, reboot.
